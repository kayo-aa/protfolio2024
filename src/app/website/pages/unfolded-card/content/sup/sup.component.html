What I'm hearing

I love music and browsing new styles. Lately I'm getting into neo-soul and hip-hop. However, my favorite bands are rock! I'll tell you about my favorite styles

Neo-Soul

Is a musical style analogous to contemporary R&B. Ms. Lauryn Hill, Mac Ayres, Anderson .Paak and Vinocio are the best I know.

Hip-Hop

Musical style in which the lyrics are more recited than sung. Mac Miller, Kanye West, Little Simz and Gorillaz are amazing!

Rock

Whether national or international, I always like the frenetic. Divididos, Arctic Monkeys, Oasis and Cerati always sound good

Funky

It's always great to dance to a good groove. With Michael Jackson, Steavie Wonder and Vulfpeck it is possible.

What I'm reading

I like to entertain myself by reading manga and novels. I like the police genre!

Narraciones Extraordinarias

It features the well-known short stories of the writer Edgar Allan Poe. I like "corazón delator"!

Oyasumi Punpun

It tells the life of Punpun from child to adult, growing up with all the problems a person can have. Kind of sad but with a beautiful drawing.

Percy Jackson

The play is set in the United States in the current era but is based on a notable event in Greek mythology.

Uzumaki

Horror manga where a small town is the victim of a curse that involves a plague of spirals that progressively affects all its inhabitants.
